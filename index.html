Biography

Hello, my name is Austin Lin, and I am a third-year student at National Tsing Hua University (NTHU). My major is Foreign Languages and Literature. I chose this major because I have always been interested in learning different languages. After almost three years in the department, I have learned much more than just foreign languages. My communication, presentation, and critical thinking skills have improved throughout my academic journey. Reading literary works from different periods has broadened my perspective and deepened my appreciation for language.

In my free time, I enjoy watching movies and listening to music. My favorite movie is Spirited Away, directed by Hayao Miyazaki. The animation, soundtrack, atmosphere, and, most importantly, the plot are all perfect in my opinion. I became interested in the film industry during my high school years. One of my friends was obsessed with movies and shared a great deal of knowledge with me, which sparked my interest in cinema. I would highly recommend a social media app called Letterboxd to all film lovers. It allows users to keep a movie-watching diary and read other people's reviews of films.

In the future, I hope to continue developing my knowledge and skills while pursuing meaningful opportunities that allow me to contribute to society and achieve my personal goals.
