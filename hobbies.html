My hobbies

My biggest hobby is music. Music has always been an important part of my life. Whether I am studying, relaxing, or traveling, I usually have my headphones on. Music is more than just entertainment to me; it is a way to express emotions, explore creativity, and connect with people from different cultures.

My musical journey began in elementary school when I started learning to play the violin. Although I stopped playing it during junior high school, the experience continued to inspire me to explore music. At first, I simply enjoyed listening to Chinese and English pop songs that were trending online. Over time, however, I began exploring different genres and artists. As my musical taste expanded, I discovered electronic dance music (EDM). I was fascinated by how producers could create unique sounds and emotional experiences using digital tools.

My favorite artist—and biggest idol—is Martin Garrix, a highly talented DJ and producer from the Netherlands. He became famous in the EDM scene when he was only sixteen years old. I admire his ability to write emotional melodies and create powerful drops. Whenever I watch his performances, whether online or in person, they always leave me amazed.

One reason I enjoy electronic music is the creativity involved in the production process. Unlike traditional music, which relies mainly on instruments, electronic music allows artists to design sounds from scratch. Producers can combine melodies, rhythms, sound effects, and technology to create something entirely new. This aspect of music inspired me to learn more about music production and the techniques behind my favorite songs.

I started learning how to DJ during my first year of college and joined the DJ club at my university. My artist name is RikoCHET. Through the club, I have had the opportunity to play music I enjoy at various events and meet people who share similar musical interests. These experiences have become some of the most memorable parts of my college life. Recently, I also started learning music production. I do not want to simply play other people's music; I want to create my own sound. I downloaded a DAW (Digital Audio Workstation) called FL studio and watched countless tutorials online. Since then, I have learned a great deal about music production and made significant progress.

One aspect of music that I value most is its ability to bring people together. Through online communities and social media platforms, I have met people who share similar musical interests. These interactions have introduced me to new artists, ideas, and perspectives that I might not have discovered on my own.

In the future, I hope to continue exploring music, learning more about production techniques, and expanding my understanding of different genres. Music has enriched my life in countless ways, and I believe it will remain one of my most meaningful interests for years to come.
